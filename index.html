Who is Jesus?

Miracles and Life of Jesus Table

Jesus is the firstborn and only son of God our savior. He died on the cross for our sins to save the world from eternal death. Jesus is great at showing us how a human being should live. In the bible we can see many stories about it and how he served as an example to the humanity. Based on the bible there is only one God, though Jesus is not God, he is a part of him like a daughter is to their mother. He is of his spirit but as a human with emotions, troubles, and temptations.

Jesus is the way, the truth and the life.
John 14:6
